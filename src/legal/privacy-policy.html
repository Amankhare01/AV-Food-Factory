Privacy Policy

At AV Food Factory, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide during bookings, such as name, email, and payment details.
  • Non-personal data like browser type, IP address, and usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your bookings and manage reservations.
  • To improve our website and customer service experience.
  • To send promotional emails, only if you opt-in.

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to process your bookings securely.

4. Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and breaches.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information at any time.
  • To exercise your rights, contact us at support@avfoodfactory.com.

6. Updates to this Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
